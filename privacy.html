Your Privacy Matters

Please Review how we collect, use and protect your data

Information We Collect

To provide our services, we collect necessary information which includes your name, email address, device information, and location (if enabled). For workers, we track tasks performed and withdrawal details. For clients, we track tasks posted and payment details.

How We Use It

Your data is used to provide account access, prevent fraud, track performance for both workers and clients, personalize your experience, and calculate rewards and payments accurately.

Data Sharing

We will never sell your personal data. It is shared only with trusted third-party service providers for essential functions, such as payment providers (e.g., bKash, Nagad) to process withdrawals, and analytics partners to help us improve our service. We may also share data if required by law.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, edit, or request the deletion of your personal data at any time. You can manage most of your information from your profile page or email us at support@chototask.com to request full data removal.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via email or an in-app notification. Please check the 'Last Updated' date for the most current version.
