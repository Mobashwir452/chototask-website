Getting Started

Learn the basics of creating an account and navigating the platform.

Welcome to ChotoTask!

This guide will walk you through the first steps to becoming a successful member of our community, whether you're here to earn or to get work done.

1. Creating Your Account

Creating an account is simple and free. Click the "Sign Up" button on our homepage. You'll be asked for your name, email, and a password. The most important step is choosing your account type:

  • Worker: Choose this if you want to complete tasks and earn money.
  • Client: Choose this if you want to post tasks for others to complete.

2. Navigating Your Dashboard

Once you log in, you will land on your personal dashboard. This is your command center. Workers will see their earnings and available tasks, while clients will see their active campaigns and submission reviews.
