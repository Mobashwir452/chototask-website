Understanding Payments

Our payment system is designed to be secure and transparent for both clients and workers.

For Clients: Depositing Funds

To post a job, you must first deposit funds into your account wallet. We support various payment methods. All funds are held securely and are only paid out to workers after you approve their work.

For Workers: Withdrawing Earnings

Your earnings are held in your secure wallet. You can request a withdrawal at any time once you meet the minimum withdrawal threshold. Simply choose your preferred payment method and submit a request.
