Account Settings

Help with updating your profile, changing passwords, and notifications.

Managing Your Account

You can manage all your account details from the "Settings" page, which is accessible from your dashboard.

Updating Your Profile

You can update your name, profile picture, and other personal information in the "Profile" section of your settings.

Changing Your Password

For security, we recommend changing your password periodically. You can do this in the "Security" section of your settings. If you forget your password, you can use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset it.
