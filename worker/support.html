Frequently Asked Questions

You can request a withdrawal from the 'Withdraw' page once your balance meets the minimum threshold. Payouts are typically processed within 48 hours.

If a submission is rejected, you will not be paid for that task. You can view the rejection reason on the submission's detail page. If you believe the rejection was unfair, you can open a support ticket from this page.

Your approval rate is the percentage of your total reviewed submissions (approved + rejected) that have been approved. A higher rate makes your profile more attractive to clients.
