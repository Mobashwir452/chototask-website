Getting Your Tasks Done

This guide explains how clients can get the most out of the ChotoTask platform.

1. Posting an Effective Job

From your dashboard, click "Post a New Job". The key to good results is clear instructions. Be very specific about what you need workers to do and what proof you require. Set a fair price per task to attract the best workers.

2. Reviewing Submissions

As workers complete your task, their submissions will appear in the "Review Submissions" section of your job management page. Carefully review the proof provided. You can either "Approve" a submission, which releases the payment to the worker, or "Reject" it if the work is incorrect. You must provide a reason for any rejection.
