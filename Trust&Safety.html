Dispute Resolution

We understand that disagreements can happen. If a worker believes their submission was unfairly rejected, or a client believes the submitted work is incorrect, either party can open a dispute. Our dedicated support team will review the case and make a final, binding decision.

Prohibited Activities

To maintain a safe environment, we strictly prohibit any activities that are illegal, fraudulent, or violate our terms of service. Accounts found to be engaging in such activities will be permanently suspended.
