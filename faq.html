Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ChotoTask.

How do I earn money here? +

You earn money by successfully completing micro-tasks posted by clients. Browse the available tasks, follow the instructions carefully, and submit the required proof. Once the client approves your work, the payment is added to your account wallet.

When will I get paid? +

Payment is credited to your ChotoTask wallet instantly after a client approves your submitted proof. From your wallet, you can request a withdrawal to your preferred payment method, which is typically processed within 24-48 hours.

What happens if my proof is rejected? +

If a client rejects your proof, they must provide a reason. In most cases, you will have an opportunity to correct your mistake and resubmit the proof. If you believe your work was unfairly rejected, you can open a dispute for our support team to review.

Benefit of premium features? +

Premium workers get access to higher-paying exclusive tasks, enjoy lower withdrawal fees, and receive priority support from our team. It's a great way to maximize your earnings on the platform.

Prices of premium? +

We offer flexible monthly and yearly subscription plans for our premium features. Please visit our 'Pricing' page for the most up-to-date information and to choose the plan that best suits you.

How do I post here? +

After signing up as a client, click the "Post a New Job" button. You will be guided through a simple form to provide a title, detailed instructions, the proof you require, and the payment per task. Once submitted, our team will review and approve it.

When will I need to pay? +

You need to deposit funds into your ChotoTask account before your task campaign can go live. We operate on a pre-paid system. Payment is only released to a worker from your account after you have personally reviewed and approved their submitted proof.

What happens if ads is rejected? +

If your task (ad) is rejected by our review team, we will provide a clear reason (e.g., unclear instructions, violation of terms). You can then edit your task to meet the guidelines and resubmit it for approval without any extra charge.

Benefit of premium features? +

Premium clients can target workers from specific regions, get detailed analytics on their campaigns, and have their tasks featured on the homepage for faster completion. You also receive priority support.

Prices of premium? +

We offer various packages for premium client features based on your needs. Please visit our 'Pricing' page for detailed information on our current plans and benefits.

Still have questions?

Our support team is here to help you with any additional questions or concerns you may have about our platform.
